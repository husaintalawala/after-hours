How it works

From “where should
we go?” to home.

Drift carries a group trip from the first spark of an idea all the way to the last settled payment — no scattered group chats, no mystery spreadsheet.

1

Start a trip

Name it, set the dates, drop a destination. Drift builds the shell of your itinerary in seconds.

2

Bring your crew

Invite everyone with a link. They see the same plan, vote on ideas, and chat inside the trip.

3

Plan & book

Compare stays, line up tours, and build the day-by-day together — with the names you trust.

4

Split as you go

Log expenses as they happen and Drift keeps the math. End the trip square, one tap to settle.
